Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved?

There are many ways to get involved with GlobalConnect. You can donate to our cause, volunteer your time and skills, or become a corporate partner. Every contribution, no matter how big or small, makes a difference.

What kind of volunteer opportunities are available?

We offer a wide range of volunteer opportunities, from hands-on work in our communities to remote support from anywhere in the world. Whether you are a student, a professional, or simply someone who wants to give back, we have a role for you. Visit our Volunteer page to learn more.

How are my donations used?

We are committed to transparency and accountability. 85% of every donation goes directly to our programs, helping us to provide education, healthcare, and economic opportunities to those in need. For a detailed breakdown of our financials, please see our latest Annual Report.

Can I direct my donation to a specific program?

Yes, you can. When you make a donation, you will have the option to direct your gift to a specific program or to our general fund, which supports all of our work. If you have any questions about how to direct your donation, please do not hesitate to contact us.
