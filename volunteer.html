Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age to volunteer?

The minimum age for most of our programs is 18. However, we have specific youth programs where high school students can get involved. Please check the specific program details.

Do I need any specific skills to volunteer?

Many of our roles require no specific skills—just a positive attitude and a willingness to help! Some roles, like mentorship or digital literacy, may require specific experience, which will be outlined in the role description.

What is the time commitment?

Time commitments vary from a few hours for a single event to a few hours per week for ongoing roles like mentorship. We are flexible and can work with your schedule.
