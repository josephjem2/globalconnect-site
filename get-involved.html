Frequently Asked Questions

What is the time commitment for volunteering?

Time commitments vary depending on the role. We have opportunities ranging from a few hours a month to more intensive, project-based work. We are flexible and work to match your availability.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! Many of our volunteer opportunities are fully remote, allowing you to contribute from anywhere in the world. Check our volunteer page for specific remote roles.

How are donations used?

We are committed to financial transparency. 85% of all donations go directly to program services. You can find detailed financial reports in our annual statements.
